There's COUNTLESS opportunities for warm prospecting and finding new clients.
Again... daily! 🙌
3. Hand-picked conversations + LinkedIn Premium auto-reply sequence.
Prospects that come to my profile from my commenting visibility get a friendly DM from me > auto-message kicks in after their response > they see my offer without me even trying to sell to them.
Either way, I talk to people, whether they buy or not. Either I get a conversion or a conversation.
Win-win. 🤝
4. Live sessions > I host a LinkedIn Live (for several hours) every quarter. These events do 3 things for me:
a. I demonstrate my live coaching style
b. They're a fast conversion engine
c. I deepen my community trust
